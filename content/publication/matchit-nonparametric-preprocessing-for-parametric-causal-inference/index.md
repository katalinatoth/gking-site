  - "Daniel Ho"
  - "Elizabeth A. Stuart"
publication_types:
  - software
abstract: |-
  MatchIt implements a wide range of sophisticated matching methods for preprocessing data before parametric causal inference, greatly reducing dependence of causal inferences on hard-to-justify modeling assumptions. After preprocessing with MatchIt, researchers can use whatever parametric model they would have used otherwise, but produce inferences with substantially more robustness and less sensitivity to modeling choices. MatchIt is an R program and also works seamlessly with Zelig.
