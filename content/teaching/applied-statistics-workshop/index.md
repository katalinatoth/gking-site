---
title: "Applied Statistics Workshop (Gov 3009)"
---

The **Applied Statistics Workshop** (Gov 3009) meets throughout the academic year on **Wednesdays, 12:00–1:30 p.m.**, in **CGIS K354** (unless otherwise announced).

The workshop is a forum for faculty, graduate students, visiting scholars, and others in the area to present and discuss methodological or empirical work in progress in an interdisciplinary setting. It features a tour of Harvard’s statistical innovations and applications, with weekly stops in different disciplines. Lunch is provided.

It is a good way to meet the community of data scientists across campus. Announcements of speakers, cancellations, and room changes are typically distributed by the host department and IQSS mailing lists rather than on a single public page; ask colleagues in Government or IQSS for the current schedule if you are on campus.
